- Inject a local React state initialized with an array of 20 realistic corporate users with diverse metrics (XP ranging from 150 to 2500, active streaks from 0 to 14 days, and statuses of "Active", "Suspect", or "Banned").

Generate the clean modular components, hook them together perfectly inside App.jsx, ensure all Tailwind configuration rules are applied, and launch the server immediately.
